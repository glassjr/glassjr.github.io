Pan Pizza


Winter 2017

I think pan pizza is the best and easiest pizza you can make at home in a residential oven. I make just about everything from scratch: the dough, the sauce and most toppings. I use store-bought low-moisture mozzeralla, though I hear homemade mozzeralla is not that hard. My goto pizzas are classic pepperoni and white sauce with carmelized onions. Check out my recipe in this blog post.
